Best Rivian R1S Accessories

Screen protectors, console organizers, roof racks, mud flaps, charging gear, and more for your R1S.

1. Tempered Glass Screen Protector

A tempered glass screen protector is one of the first accessories R1S owners should install. The large infotainment display is prone to fingerprints and scratches. Multiple brands offer 9H hardness tempered glass cut specifically for the Rivian display.

Pros

  • 9H hardness protection
  • Anti-fingerprint coating
  • Anti-glare options available
  • Custom cut for Rivian screen

Cons

  • Installation requires care
  • May affect touch sensitivity slightly

2. Center Console Organizer Tray

The R1S center console is spacious but can become a catch-all. Custom-fit organizer trays add compartments for phones, cards, keys, and sunglasses. Most are made from silicone or ABS plastic with a flocked or rubberized finish.

Pros

  • Custom-fit for R1S console
  • Multiple compartments
  • Easy to remove and clean
  • Non-slip material

Cons

  • Reduces total console depth
  • Quality varies by brand

3. Mud Flaps / Splash Guards

Custom mud flaps protect the R1S body panels from road debris, mud, and rock chips — especially important for an adventure-oriented vehicle. Most options bolt on without drilling using existing mounting points.

Pros

  • Protects paint from debris
  • No-drill installation (most)
  • Durable TPE or rubber
  • Front and rear sets available

Cons

  • May affect ground clearance slightly
  • Aesthetic preference varies

4. Roof Rack Cross Bars

The R1S comes with factory roof rails, and aftermarket cross bars let you mount cargo boxes, bike racks, kayak carriers, and more. Look for aluminum alloy bars with aerodynamic profiles to minimize wind noise and drag.

Pros

  • Fits factory roof rails
  • Aluminum construction
  • Enables cargo carriers/bike racks
  • Aerodynamic options available

Cons

  • Added weight affects range slightly
  • Wind noise at highway speeds

While the R1S comes with a mobile charger, many owners upgrade to a faster Level 2 portable charger or add NEMA 14-50 adapters for flexibility when traveling. Brands like Lectron and Grizzl-E offer popular options.

Pros

  • Faster home charging
  • Portable for travel
  • Multiple plug adapters
  • Works with standard outlets

Cons

  • Requires appropriate electrical circuit
  • Quality varies significantly

LED accent light strips can enhance the R1S cabin ambiance. Many kits connect via USB and offer app-controlled colors and patterns. Look for kits designed for automotive use with proper adhesion and heat tolerance.

Pros
